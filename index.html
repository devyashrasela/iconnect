About the Conference

The International Conference on Electrical, Communication, and Computing Technologies (iCONECCT-2025) provides a platform for experts, researchers, and professionals to present pioneering research, explore technological advancements, and discuss innovative solutions in the fields of electrical, communication, and computing technologies.
This event promotes cross-disciplinary collaboration, encourages the exchange of ideas, and offers valuable insights into emerging trends. It includes keynote speeches by distinguished experts and interactive technical sessions to enrich the experience of all participants.
Prospective authors are encouraged to contribute and help shape the conference by submitting original and unpublished research papers. Contributions can include conceptual, empirical, experimental, or theoretical work in relevant areas.
